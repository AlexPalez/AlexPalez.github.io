PHOTO GALLERY

MT MAUNGANUI
Mt Maunganui
Mt Maunganui beaches and Moturiki island
Mt Maunganui is a place in Tauranga that is very popular in the summer time. The mountain shape is a result of a volcano over 2 million years ago, which is now inactive and remains an iconic attraction for those to see and even climb up to see the view from the top. The beaches in Mt Maunganui are known to be ideal in the summer time, as the water is very warm and the sand is perfect for families to picnic and spend days on end there.
ROTORUA & TAUPO
Rotorua and Taupo are iconic locations that are located in the heart of the North Island. These images capture the essence of New Zealand's natural beauty, from the Rotorua canopy tour through the tree-top bridges and ziplines, to the Huka falls and the river running through Taupo.
MAHIA
Mahia is a beach town. For those who love beaches, Mahia is the place to go as the views from the top of the hill are 360 degrees, with warm, blue waters all around. It is the perfect getaway location, and one of my favourite places in New Zealand.
HAWKES BAY
Hawkes Bay is a region on the west coast of the North Island. This region is considered the ideal family location, with a good balance between townships and untouched nature, as well as scorching heat in the summertime. These photos are only some of the best locations in the Hawkes Bay region but there are plenty more sites to see.
WELLINGTON
Wellington is the capital of New Zealand. It is a city that has plenty of nature, as well as the industrial sector and a large CBD all mixed together. Wellington is a good city to live in, with a good balance between nature and a fully functioning city.
